❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📦 How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within India. Express shipping options are available at checkout. Orders are processed within 24 hours of placement.
📦 Do you ship internationally? +
Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when it launches.
🚚 Can I track my order? +
Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also check the status in your Order History page on our website.
🔄 What is your return policy? +
We offer easy returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and in original condition. Free return pickup available in most areas.
💰 How long do refunds take? +
Once we receive and verify your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. It may take an additional 2-3 business days for the amount to reflect in your account.
🔄 What if the product is damaged or defective? +
Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost. Damaged items can be picked up from your location.
💳 What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All payment methods are secure and encrypted for your safety.
🔒 Is my payment information safe? +
Yes, your payment information is completely safe. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. Your details are never stored on our servers.
❌ Can I change my payment method after placing an order? +
If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately and we can help change the payment method. Once shipped, you'll need to complete the original transaction.
👕 What are the fabric details? +
Each product page contains detailed fabric information. We use premium quality materials including silk, cotton blends, and traditional Indian fabrics. Detailed care instructions are provided with each item.
📏 How do I know my size? +
Each product page has a size guide. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size chart. Free exchanges are available if the size doesn't fit.
🎨 Can I customize or get alterations? +
Custom orders and personalization are available for bulk orders. Contact us at reshmafashion@gmail.com for customization inquiries.
